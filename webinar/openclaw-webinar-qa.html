What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is a hackable framework for building AI agents with their own personality, that talk through Telegram, WhatsApp, and Slack, have direct access to your computer and files, and can modify themselves as they work.

Let's unpack it.

Five levels of autonomy

The difference is how much control you keep.

Level 1

Operator

ChatGPT. Claude. Gemini. Perplexity.

“Help me write a reply to this email.”
Level 2

Collaborator

NotebookLM. ChatGPT Projects. Gemini in Docs.

“Help me sort these emails into reply, archive, and ignore.”
Level 3

Consultant

OpenClaw that takes the lead and checks in.

“Read my inbox, suggest priorities, and ask me what to do.”
Level 4

Approver

OpenClaw that runs the workflow and stops for approval.

“Read my inbox, draft replies, and stop only for approval.”
Level 5

Observer

Full autonomy. You mostly monitor.

“Handle my inbox end to end while I only review exceptions.”
Shorter, simpler
Longer, more complex
Rule of thumb: start at Operator. Most people should live between Operator and Approver.
